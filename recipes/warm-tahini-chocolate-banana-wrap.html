Warm Tahini Chocolate Banana Wrap

Click to be taken to the original source!

This warm and gooey tahini, chocolate and banana wrap is easy-to-make and delicious. It's a great quick snack, wholesome enough to enjoy for breakfast while also tasting like dessert!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp (30 mL) tahini
  • 1 Tbsp (15 mL) maple syrup
  • 1 Tbsp (7 g) Dutch processed cocoa powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 medium soft tortilla
  • 1 Tbsp (8 g) roasted unsalted hazelnuts, chopped

Optional toppings

  • fresh mixed berries
  • powdered sugar

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the tahini, maple syrup, and cocoa powder.
  2. Slice the banana in half, then into strips, lengthwise.
  3. Spread the tahini mixture evenly on the tortilla and then place the tortilla on a large pan on medium heat. Top with the banana slices and chopped hazelnuts.
  4. When the tortilla starts to become golden, gently fold the tortilla in half and press down with the back of a spatula. You can also roll the filled tortilla into a burrito and toast it in the pan for a neater "to-go" option.
  5. Remove the tortilla from the pan, cut it in half, and add your desired toppings. Enjoy!
